Wheat Flours for the Home


San Antonio wheat flour

The Official Mexican Standard defines wheat flour; to obtaining the milling of wheat from the mature, whole, broken, healthy and dry grain of the genus tritricum, L; of the species T. vulgares, T. compactum and T. durum or mixtures of these, clean, healthy in which a large part of the bran and germ is removed and the rest is crushed until a grain of adequate fineness is obtained.


San Antonio wheat flour contains a high percentage of protein, this level of protein allows our flour to absorb 60% more water, therefore it absorbs less fat, this property also allows it to support or load several ingredients that when mixing the flour it maintains its cooking properties, texture, moisture, flavor, flexibility and sponginess, the best example is: concrete vs cement, both are used for construction but their molecular structures are very different, concrete must support large amounts of weight while cement does not. A low protein flour will absorb more fat.

Whole Wheat Flours



Tres Estrellas® whole wheat flour is added with vitamins and minerals (thiamin, riboflavin, niacin, folic acid, iron and zinc), it is also enriched with natural wheat fiber (Sanitized bran-salvadillo mixture). It is characterized by its high water absorption (>61%), which functionally allows using less product and/or capturing more water, reducing fat absorption.
