Who are we

Harinera Los Pirineos, a subsidiary of Grupo La Moderna, is the largest mill in Mexico in the production of ready-made flour for home and industrial consumption and one of the most important buyers of wheat production in Mexico.

The production of our products is divided into six market segments:

  • Wheat flour
  • Prepared flours
  • Improvers for baking
  • Baking powder
  • Wheat derivatives
  • Flours for Home
Our high-tech and high-capacity production lines can deliver finished products in any presentation from 50g to 1-tonne bulk bags.

The manufacture of our flours is carried out in state-of-the-art equipment that allows us to comply with the highest international standards of quality, safety, security, legality and authenticity. (BRCGS, C-TPAT, FDA/FSMA, CRESE, Kosher logos).



Evolution / Chronology



TABLEX group acquires the assets of Sociedad Molinos y Campesinos S.A. de C.V., thus Harinera Los Pirineos, S.A. was born. of C.V.
1988


The Abasolo winery is built, Gto., Zone of high wheat production in the Bajío
1991


Grupo La Moderna acquires La Montaña, S.A. of C.V. located in Irapuato, Gto.
1996


OPISECSA merges with Harinera Los Pirineos, giving rise to the grain receiving center of Villagrán, Gto
2001


Grupo La Moderna acquires the Tres Estrellas brand, with the Harinera Los Pirineos plant being responsible for the development and manufacture of flours prepared under this brand.
2002


Harinera Los Pirineos modernizes its entire wheat milling process in the u classad of Salamanca and Irapuato. The Research and Development area is created.
2004


The u classad of continuous mixtures of white flour is built with which Harinera Los Pirineos is placed inside the mills with cutting-edge technology, which allows the manufacture of recipes tailored to the client's needs.
2008


The flour plant prepared with the highest technology available in terms of mixing, packing and packaging is built. We became the first wheat mill with an integrated prepared flour plant.
2009


Packaging automation is carried out, through the installation of modern machinery in all its lines.
2010


The Pyrenees Distribution Center is built under the most advanced standards in raw material storage, packaging and finished product.
2011


A third milling class is installed for soft wheat.
2013


A manufacturing line for food grade rolled wheat is installed. The fire network is installed. Automation of the 44 kg flour load.
2014


High veil classad packaging equipment for 1 kg flour is installed. The microbiology laboratory is expanded. The heat-based pest control system called Thermonox is acquired.
2015


The infrastructure for the manufacture of food grade wheat by-product is strengthened.
2016


The expansion project begins in the grinding classad layer of the Irapuato Plant.
2017


New expansion and remodeling of the U classad of Irapuato comes into operation. The La Moderna Railway project begins in Villagrán, Gto.
2018


The La Moderna Uwe Schwarz Railway begins operations in Villagrán, Gto.
2019


The reverse osmosis plant is installed in the Salamanca plant, for the treatment of process water.
