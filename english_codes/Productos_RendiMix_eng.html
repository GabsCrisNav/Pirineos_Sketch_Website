In search of the best


RendiMix adapts bakery flours to ensure quality sponginess and consistency, in addition to offering a well-structured crust in pre-baked bread, as well as guaranteeing a longer shelf life for the final product.


Designed for your needs


Manufactured according to customer needs, RendiMix offers you the comfort and security of obtaining a dough to obtain a product with greater texture, a more consistent and lasting aroma as well as a greater yield for the final product.


RendiMix offers 2 presentations that focus on improving various qualities of the dough to be made, such as giving greater volume and a crispy and durable crust, however, each presentation of RendiMix seeks to make the mass stand out for something that makes it unique.





Unmatched efficiency


Selected with the greatest care, the constituents are carefully codified to establish optimal efficacy and are diluted on a flour support, making RendiMix facilitate its use at the time of kneading.


Simple, consistent and high quality


RendiMix controls all the uncertainties within the bread production process (temperature, humidity, flour, etc.) and ensures first-class and consistent results in each batch that is baked. RendiMix will help you overcome production challenges and ensure high-quality products.


An unparalleled view, texture and flavor


At the level of the final product, it improves the quality of the bread with respect to the texture of the crumb, the color and appearance of the crust, the volume, the conservation and the taste.


Tasty and healthy


Add RendiMix to your bakery dough is essential to achieve breads that are delicious with the least possible effort. The mixture of RendiMix was selected with extreme care because we believe that improving your dough needs healthy things.
