Made with the best

Bakery Mix,It is made with the most innovative technology, making an exact mixture of the best ingredients and flour from the highest quality wheat grains, which makes each manufactured premix special and unique.


Custom Built

Manufactured according to customer needs, Bakery Mix shortens production time, in addition to providing the best characteristics to the products, such as a uniform or fluffy crumb (depending on use), smooth texture, long shelf life, freshness, firm structure and excellent flavor.


Bakery Mix offers premixes for the following specialties: donuts, biscuits, danish bread, dead bread, king's cake, buns, white or wholemeal box bread, pizza, crepes, chocolate or vanilla sponge cake, chocolate or vanilla American cake, pancake chocolate or vanilla, chocolate or vanilla muffin and churros.





Integral solution


Being manufactured according to the precise specifications of the client, Bakery Mix allows saving in sugar, fats, flavorings and emulsifiers, since the waste and leftovers that are generated in the process of mixing these ingredients are avoided. Likewise, it allows optimal inventory management, as it helps to standardize the quality and quantity of bread produced.


Speed, performance and quality


Bakery Mix is synonymous with speed by saving time in preparation and weighing of ingredients, kneading and baking times of the bread, since it makes each gram of dough yield, giving it the best amount in the final products.


Preserve the traditional flavor


Being a specialized premix for each type of bread and with total quality control from the origin of the wheat, Bakery Mix allows standardizing the preparation of bread, always achieving a uniform product that preserves the unique flavor of your bakery in each batch.


Tasty and healthy


Stock your bakery with Bakery Mix is essential to achieve healthy recipes. We manufacture it with the best quality ingredients, because we believe that eating rich is also eating healthy.
