Our Three Great Pillars

1. Technology and Infrastructure

Research and Development Laboratories

In the laboratory of chemical and biological aspects of the flours, the lots must be validated to be delivered and thus we keep historical samples of the production for up to a year, what we call "DNA of the flour".


The functional evaluation laboratory includes the bakery laboratory, where daily production samples of white flour and flour prepared from each shift are taken, in order to verify the final quality of the bread already made.


Cleaning team

Modernization and strict control in the cleaning system for wheat by-products, food grade.


Grinding equipment

Technology in all the equipment and grinding process, both in the Salamanca and Irapuato units.


Mixing equipment

Our prepared flour plant has an installed capacity of 3,500 tons per month. We have storage capacity for *2,200 tons* of white flour and the most advanced continuous mixing system, we can satisfy any flour requirement.


Packaging equipment

State-of-the-art production lines and automation with high-speed packaging from 50 g to 1-tonne bulk bags.


Distribution Center / CEDI

With an area of 2,400 m2 and 6,000 storage positions, it allows us to safely and efficiently manage all our supplies and finished products.


Ecological treatments

Thermonox: Treatments in which pests in any of their stages of development that affect cereals are safely exterminated at a temperature of approximately 50·C by protein coagulation.


CO2: Technology in the experimental phase in which the oxygen in the storage silo is displaced by a mixture of Carbon Dioxide and Nitrogen, causing the death of pests by suffocation.


Wastewater treatment plant: through physical, chemical and biological processes, all the organic matter present in the effluent water of the plant is eliminated. The treated water is used for irrigation in green areas.


We have four work centers located in the State of Guanajuato

  • Planta Salamanca
  • Planta Irapuato
  • Ferropuerto Uwe Schwarz Villagrán
  • Bodega Abasolo

This infrastructure allows our logistics tasks to meet the level of service expected by our customers.


2. Quality Systems

In the year 2000, we began the quality management process under the AIB International standard, maintaining the audit system in the following years with favorable results.


In 2010, with the arrival of new and important national and international clients, Harinera Los Pirineos took on the challenge of being certified under the BRC Standard, being the first mill in Mexico with this certification and maintaining this system ever since.


We have the C-TPAT certification to guarantee the security and integrity of the supply chain in exports to the United States, which also provides us with a reduction in border crossing times.

3. Our People

Schooling System

As an important commitment incorporated into the strategies that set the course of the company, we achieved the “Company with Socio-Labor Responsibility” Certification from the Government of the State of Guanajuato.


In an agreement with SEG, we started the Upper Secondary Education System for Sunday workers, granting economic incentives for progress in their studies. The first generation of 68 graduates concluded in 2016 and the second with 55 in March 2017.


We provide support in scholarships and flexibility of schedules for trusted personnel who are pursuing a degree.


Dual training / Pyrenees Classroom

An agreement was recently signed between the Pyrenees, the Employers' Confederation of the Mexican Republic and the National College of Technical Professional Education, which allows students to spend the last 3 semesters of their training 80% of the time in the company and 20% in Classroom.


Contract farming

Mechanism of the Federal Government for the development of agriculture in the area, executed by ASERCA and guaranteeing the purchase price to the producer; In this program both producers and Harinera Los Pirineos take coverage where the premium is partially supported by ASERCA, benefiting 10,000 farmers in the region.


Support Casa Hogar Alegra Salamanca

Monthly attention to the children of the home, through gatherings and activities integrated by crews of personnel and support in kind, as well as in the service of the institution.
